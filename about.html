About Stock Analyzer

The website, Stock Analyzer, helps the users decide whether to invest in a company by providing stock information and related articles for that company. Our goal is to provide quick access to stock related information so the website may aid our users in making well-thoughtout investments.


The data for our financial information (e.g. stock chart) is from Yahoo Finance. For related articles and current news, we retrieved the data from New York Times. Also we give credit to TechanJS for integrated their d3 graph.
